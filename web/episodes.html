About the show

The NDA Club started as a daily phone call between two ex colleagues, talking about ideas each of them came across during their jobs. Anthony is a freelance Backend engineer, getting pitched projects on a daily basis that want him as their CTO, Benjamin is works as an investor for a European VC Firm. One day, they agreed that their conversations might be interesting to be published, so they bought 2 mics as started recording their conversations.
